Eco-Friendly


Quality Dry Cleaners take pride with their Eco-Friendly services. We use all Eco-Friendly products and wish to provide quality services for you.
We use wet cleaning machines since dry cleaning has a solvent called Perchloroethylene (“perc”). This chemical can be hazardous, so we don't use it.
